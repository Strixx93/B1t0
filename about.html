Our Story :)

We’re not just a team—we’re a crew of curious minds, creative builders, and late-night tinkerers powered by coffee, code, and a love for all things robotic.

Based in the heart of Sebeș, Romania, we’re a robotics team driven by passion, precision, and a shared vision of building a smarter future—one motor, sensor, and line of code at a time. Whether we’re soldering wires in our workshop or brainstorming over pizza, we’re always working to push boundaries and bring ideas to life.

What started as a group of students with a wild dream of making robots that actually work (well... mostly) has grown into a tight-knit team competing, learning, and growing together. We believe in the power of teamwork, the thrill of innovation, and the beauty of failure—because every error brings us closer to getting it right.

We’re here to represent Sebeș on the map of tech and innovation, inspire the next generation of engineers, and—let’s be honest—have a ton of fun building robots that can out-think, out-move, and out-wow the competition.

Welcome to our world.

The robots are just getting started.
